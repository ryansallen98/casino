Privacy Policy

At iCore Payments, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy applies to all information that we collect through our website, including personal information submitted through our contact form.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from users through our contact form, which may include name, email address, phone number, subject, and message content. This information is used to respond to user inquiries and to provide customer support.

Use of Personal Information

The personal information we collect through our website is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send users relevant information about our products and services

Sharing of Personal Information

We do not share personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to provide our services.

Security of Personal Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or internet transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

Retention of Personal Information

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on our website.
