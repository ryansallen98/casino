How iCore Payments Works

1.

Self-minting BUX tokens

To use iCore Payments, a buyer can purchase authorization codes using popular methods such as credit cards and PayPal. The buyer can then self-mint and send BUX tokens to recipients, who receive the tokens directly from the buyer.

2.

Fiat Payout

The recipients can then send proof of burn for a fiat payout which can be received via USDT, PayPal, wire/ACH and other methods in future.

3.

Scalability and Security

The solution is hyper-scalable and can handle thousands of transactions per second, making it efficient for both tiny microtransactions and massive payments. The solution also benefits from the unmatched security and decentralization of Bitcoin networks.
