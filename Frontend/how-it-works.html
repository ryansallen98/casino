How it Works

iCore Payments is designed to streamline the process of collecting, managing, and distributing funds. Using our self-minted eToken, BUX, on the XEC network, businesses can easily purchase authorization codes and self-mint BUX tokens to send to recipients. Recipients receive the tokens directly from the buyer and have the option to exchange them for fiat currency using popular methods such as USDT, PayPal, and wire/ACH. Our solution is hyper-scalable, efficient for tiny microtransactions or massive payments, and built on the proven security of Bitcoin networks.

STEP 1

Purchase Authorization Codes

To use iCore Payments, a buyer can purchase authorization codes using popular methods such as credit cards and PayPal. The authorization codes are used to mint BUX tokens.

STEP 2

Self-mint BUX Tokens

Once the authorization codes have been purchased, the buyer can self-mint BUX tokens. The tokens are then sent to the recipients' addresses.

STEP 3

Send Tokens to Recipients

Recipients will receive the BUX tokens directly from the buyer. They can then use the tokens for transactions or exchange them for fiat currency.

STEP 4

Request Payout

Recipients can then send proof of burn for a fiat payout which can be received via USDT, PayPal, wire/ACH and other methods in future. The payout will be processed in near real-time.

Note: iCore payments is built on Bitcoin networks and is fully compliant with regulations. It is a trustless and decentralized solution. It also benefits from the unmatched security of Bitcoin networks.
