Frequently Asked Questions.

What is iCore Payments?

iCore Payments is a revolutionary new payment solution that utilizes BUX, a Bitcoin-based eToken on the XEC network, to provide instant and secure multi-recipient payments for businesses and recipients worldwide.

What are the benefits of using iCore Payments?

iCore Payments can help businesses increase revenue and profits, eliminate and lower costs, reduce overhead, attract partners, secure transactions, address regulatory matters, improve customer service, and easily receive and make global payments.

How does iCore Payments compare to other payment solutions?

iCore Payments provides faster, cheaper, and more secure payments than traditional payment systems and cryptocurrencies, with a self-minting protocol that eliminates fraud and breakage.

How can I contact iCore Payments?

You can contact iCore Payments by email, or through the contact form on our website. Our customer support team is happy to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
