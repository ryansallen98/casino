The Benifits

iCore Payments is built on Bitcoin networks and is fully compliant with regulations.
It is a trustless and decentralized solution.

What's the difference?

Legacy System

Inefficiency: The process of collecting, managing, and distributing funds can be laborious and time-consuming, leading to delays and increased costs.
Limited Visibility: Recipients, such as creators, affiliates, suppliers, developers, sales teams, licensors, etc. often have limited visibility or transparency into their payments, leading to uncertainty and mistrust.
Risk: Central parties collecting, managing and distributing funds are typically at their own expense and risk, which can be a stressful and costly process.
Fraud: There can be issues with fraudulent activity, such as brands spending money with affiliates and not getting the results they were promised.
Lack of trustless payments: The recipient do not have trustless payments, as they wait for weeks or months for their payments with limited or no visibility.
Lack of scalability: Legacy payment systems may not be able to handle large scale transactions.
Regulatory compliance: Legacy payment systems may not be compliant with regulations.

iCore Payments

Efficiency: iCore provides instant and secure multi-recipient payments, making it a streamlined process for businesses to collect, manage, and distribute funds.
Visibility: iCore ensures that recipients have access to their payments and transaction information, allowing them to easily track and manage their earnings.
Risk: iCore is built on the proven cryptographic security of Bitcoin networks, which helps to reduce risk for businesses and recipients.
Fraud: iCore's payment solution is designed to prevent fraudulent activity and provide trust and security for businesses and recipients.
Trustless payments: iCore provides trustless payments to recipients, they can receive payments immediately with every transaction.
Scalability: iCore's solution is hyper-scalable, able to handle thousands of transactions per second, making it efficient for tiny microtransactions or massive payments.
Regulatory compliance: iCore is fully compliant with regulations, it helps businesses to manage increased regulation of money transmitters and crypto.
